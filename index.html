📖 Game Storyboard & Game Design

Step 1: Introduction (Welcome) Collect user's first name to customize their completion certificate. Set the stage for the Philippine-themed Adjectives Archery.
Step 2: Level 1 & 2 - Classic Match Connecting nouns (quiver/bows) to adjectives (targets). A direct click shooting system. Teaches basics (e.g., Mango is Sweet, Carabao is Strong).
Step 3: Level 3 - Drag and Drop Physical drag-and-drop mechanism. Drag noun items (like Lechon) into correct adjective baskets (like Crispy). Tests dexterity and speed.
Step 4: Level 4 - Physical Bow (Still Targets) Aim and shoot simulator! Pull and release the bow to target static adjectives correctly matching the noun.
Step 5: Level 5 - Moving Target Challenge The final test of coordination and linguistics! The adjective targets slide up and down continuously. Release the bow at the precise moment to hit the correct target.
Step 6: Completion Certificate Display a colorful certificate celebrating their achievements with confetti, final score, and their personalized name!
